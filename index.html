Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers for macOS users

Is PetReminder free?
The base app is free to download. Some premium pets are available via in-app purchase to support development.
Does PetReminder work offline?
Yes. Reminders and pet animations work fully offline. Only pet downloads from IAP require an internet connection.
What pets are available?
Currently includes cats (Ragdoll, British Shorthair) and dogs (Shiba Inu, Corgi, Husky). More pets are planned!
Which macOS versions are supported?
PetReminder supports macOS 12 Monterey and later, optimized for Apple Silicon (M-series).
What permissions does PetReminder need?
PetReminder runs with minimal permissions. Notifications are required for reminders. Optional permissions for custom features.
